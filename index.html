We are a one-stop destination for all your study abroad needs. We provide Online training (in GRE, SAT, GMAT, ACT, LSAT, PSAT, IELTS, and TOEFL) and counseling services to students for a global career. We also help students in all admission related queries. Our goal is not only to assist students in achieving high scores in these exams, but also to help them in the entire journey of securing admissions to their dream university. We provide the entire gamut of services: writing the exam, shortlisting colleges and courses, writing SoPs (Statement of Purpose), writing LORs (Letters of Recommendation), visa counseling, networking in the foreign country, etc., making your journey to a global career a smooth one.In our highly interactive online classes, we constantly work through each of our students to ensure that they exceed their expectations. We constantly motivate our students to work on their weak areas. To keep the engagement level high, we keep every batch restricted to a small number (ideally 4-5 students per batch only). We also provide individual classes and counselling to students for more focussed and highly effective tutoring. For the same reasons, our legacy is replete with students' testimonials and accolades.
An important aspect of counseling is finding the right fit for your child. Identifying the right college and the right course can be a daunting task. We help you in every step of making a decision in this regard. We will make sure that a strong application is submitted so that the chances of securing admission in your dream college is increased many folds. We make sure that your child gets selected for coveted programs across top colleges in the country of his/her choice. Our primary focus is to assist in preparing an impeccable Statement of Purpose (SoP) that captures the strength of your child. We provide guidance in writing illustrious LoRs (Letters of Recommendation). We do this by conducting a thorough assessment of your child's profile and by preparing an application that captures it. In the process, we map the skill set of your child with the program requirements. Based on the skill-requirement mapping and a series of personal interviews, the drafts of application documents are prepared. Applications are made to carefully thought mix of schools, including ambitious options like IVY Leagues.
